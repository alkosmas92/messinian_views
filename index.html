Agios Andreas

Peacefull Seaside Village

Agios Andreas which is located in Messinia, Peloponnese, Greece, is a picturesque fishing village. Walk towards the sea and arrive at the boulevard and the cosy harbour. Here you'll find mostly locals; the fishermen who are early in the morning picking their nets and in the evening they can be found in local tavernas. Here you will find few foreign tourists. The village takes its name from the Byzantine church of the 12th century, located on the road to the village of Logga.

Koroni

Medevial Sites

Koroni is a tiny port town on the Bay of Messinia at the Peloponnese in Greece. It is located fifty kilometres southwest of Kalamata. It is a picturesque town situated on a narrow peninsula with a colossal Venetian castle. A great option for a nice walk, sightseeing, access to beautiful beaches, drinks and food. Many foreign tourists select many years Koroni for their holidays for all the aspects that are making the destination magical!

Chrani

Beach Front Village

Located in the district of Messinia in the Peloponnese, Greece, is a small holiday village. It is located on the coast with a vast sand / pebble beach. It is a quiet village with excellent eateries including Tavern "The Garden" (“O Kipos” in Greek), the cosy bar Poseidon where until late in the evening cocktails, coffees and snacks are available, a beach tent with complimentary beach chairs, souvenir shop and some supermarkets. Chrani has no real centre but small streets with lots of flowers and the hospitality of the locals make you feel right at home.

Noupeli Views
